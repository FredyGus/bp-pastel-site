Blue Drink

Cold beverage

Refreshing with a citrus touch. Perfect for hot days.

Prices

RegularQ12.00
With gummiesQ15.00

Ingredients

  • Crushed ice
  • Blue syrup
  • Lemon juice
  • Lemon soda
  • Lemon slice

Recipe

  1. Fill the glass with ice up to 3/4.
  2. Add blue syrup (approx. 30 ml).
  3. Squeeze 10–15 ml of lemon juice.
  4. Fill with lemon soda and stir gently.
  5. Garnish with a lemon slice. Optional: add gummies.
