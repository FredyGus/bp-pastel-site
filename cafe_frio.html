Iced Coffee

Cold beverage

Espresso-based coffee with milk and ice. Creamy, refreshing, and perfect for the afternoon.

Prices

RegularQ15.00
With whipped creamQ18.00

Ingredients

  • 1–2 shots of espresso
  • Cold milk
  • Ice
  • Sugar or syrup (optional)
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Recipe

  1. Fill a glass with ice.
  2. Add freshly brewed espresso.
  3. Sweeten to taste with sugar or syrup.
  4. Top with cold milk and stir gently.
  5. Optional: finish with whipped cream.
