Lemonade

Cold beverage

Classic homemade lemonade, fresh and perfectly balanced between sweet and tart. Great at any time of day.

Prices

LargeQ15.00
SmallQ10.00

Ingredients

  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Cold water
  • Sugar or simple syrup
  • Ice
  • Lemon slices for garnish

Recipe

  1. Dissolve sugar in a bit of warm water (simple syrup) or use sugar directly.
  2. Mix lemon juice with cold water and sweeten to taste.
  3. Serve in a glass filled with plenty of ice.
  4. Garnish with lemon slices and, if desired, mint leaves.
