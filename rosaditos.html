Pink Drink

Cold beverage

Sweet and fruity with a vibrant pink color. Perfect for those who enjoy mild flavors.

Prices

RegularQ12.00
With gummiesQ15.00

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • Strawberry syrup
  • Lemon juice
  • Lime soda
  • Gummies / chamoy (optional)

Recipe

  1. Add ice to the glass.
  2. Pour about 30 ml of strawberry syrup.
  3. Squeeze a touch of lemon juice.
  4. Top with soda and stir gently.
  5. Garnish with gummies or a chamoy rim.
